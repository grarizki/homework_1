Mike Grzesiek

The Best Aimer in The World

Here's a life and work of Mike "Shroud" Grzesiek:

  • 1994 - Born in Mississauga, Ontario, Canada
  • 2013-2014 - Playing CS:GO for Slow Motion
  • 2014 - He Moved to Exertus eSports
  • 2014 - Moved again to play with Manajumas, where he started to get recognised from many NA players
  • 2014 - First trial with Complexity Gaming, form a team alongside N0thing, Hiko, sgares, Semphis
  • 2014 - He leave Complexity to join Cloud9
  • 2014 - Started to play competitively with bigger stage and prize pool
  • 2014 - He played CEVO Season 5 Professional, when the Grand Final he makes the infamous "Shroud Box" plays
  • 2017 - He stepped down from cs:go team, because he lost motivation and started to play other new and upcoming Battle Royale game, called PUBG.
  • 2018 - He left Cloud9 and retire from playing competitive CS:GO
